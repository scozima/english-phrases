# 10. Because

## 質問
Because [reason]

## 回答
Because [your reason]

## 会話例
A: Why did you choose this university?
B: Because it has an excellent computer science program.

## 使用上の注意点
- 理由や説明を述べるときに使う表現です
- 一般的なパターン：
  - "Because I want to..."
  - "Because I need to..."
  - "Because I like..."
  - "Because it's..."
- 同様の意味で使える表現：
  - "Since I want to..."
  - "As I need to..."
- フォーマルな文章では：
  - "due to"
  - "owing to"
  も使えます

## 関連する表現
- "The reason is..." (理由は...)
- "That's why..." (だから...)
- "For this reason..." (この理由で...)
