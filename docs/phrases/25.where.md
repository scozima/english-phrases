# 25. Where?

## 質問
Where [action]?

## 回答
[Location].

## 会話例
A: Where is the nearest station?
B: The nearest station is two blocks away.
A: Thank you for the directions.

## 使用上の注意点
- 場所を尋ねる一般的な表現です
- より丁寧な尋ね方：
  - "Could you tell me where it is?"
  - "Would you mind telling me the location?"
- より具体的な尋ね方：
  - "Where can I find it?"
  - "Where is it located?"
- 場所を伝える時の表現：
  - "It's at [location]"
  - "It's in [area]"
  - "It's near [landmark]"

## 関連する表現
- "Which way?" (どの方向ですか？)
- "How do I get there?" (どうやって行けばいいですか？)
- "Is it far?" (遠いですか？)
